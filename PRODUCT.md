# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

BitGates serves three primary audiences: Simplified Chinese speaking HKU students and alumni who want a credible Web3 learning and networking community; English speaking clients, enterprises, and ecosystem partners assessing collaboration quality; industry advisors and guests who may support research, talks, mentorship, and institutional connections.

## Product Purpose

The website introduces HKU BitGates Society as an alumni-initiated, nonprofit Web3 evangelism and research community founded by HKU students and alumni in 2025. It must translate a PDF introduction into a credible bilingual digital presence that demonstrates community traction, institutional seriousness, program structure, team quality, governance, and partnership readiness.

## Brand Personality

Scholarly, deliberate, networked. BitGates should feel like a rare energy field formed by young long-termists, not a speculative crypto campaign. The brand voice is confident, rational, altruistic, and future-facing.

## Anti-references

Do not look like a neon crypto launchpad, token sale page, casino trading platform, generic SaaS landing page, or student club flyer. Avoid dark purple gradients, meme coin aesthetics, fake institutional language, and empty Web3 buzzwords.

## Design Principles

1. Credibility before spectacle: show evidence, programs, people, and governance before making claims.
2. Bilingual by design: Chinese and English readers should both feel the site was written for them, not machine-translated afterward.
3. Academic gravity with industry delivery: pair university seriousness with partner-ready clarity.
4. Long-termism over hype: frame Web3 as infrastructure, research, and production relationship change, not speculation.
5. Nodes over cards: express the organization as a living network of people, programs, companies, and ideas.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard navigability, clear focus states, reduced motion support, bilingual language controls, readable body line lengths, and responsive layouts for phone, tablet, and desktop.
